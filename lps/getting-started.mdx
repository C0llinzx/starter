As a liquidity provider, you deposit USDC to underwrite verified trading managers and earn carry on the profits they generate. This is a new asset class: direct on-chain participation in the performance of verified, multi-venue trading managers.

<Warning>
Hyro vaults are a **high-risk allocation**. Returns are tied to live trader PnL — not a fixed yield, not a synthetic basis trade, not a lending APR. Expect drawdowns. See [Risk Disclosure](/lps/risk).
</Warning>

## How it works in one paragraph

You deposit USDC into a vault and receive **vault shares** minted at the current NAV. Your capital is routed to the manager's Bybit sub-account; the manager trades; you earn carry on profits. When you withdraw, shares are burned and USDC returns at the prevailing NAV — with a high-water-mark-protected performance fee crystallizing on realized gains.
