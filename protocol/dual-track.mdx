The dual-track model is Hyro Protocol's flagship mechanic. It removes two things that make traditional prop firms frustrating: the **artificial graduation event** and the **demo cancellation** that usually comes with it.

## The problem it solves

On most prop firms, hitting a profit target triggers a single binary moment — you "graduate," your old account is closed, and you start over on new terms. The scaling is artificial and the relationship is transactional.

Hyro replaces that with two parallel tracks that open simultaneously at **+15%**.

## What happens at +15%

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Track A — Demo continues (Tier 2)" icon="repeat">
    Your demo account is **not** canceled. You keep trading it at a reduced **50/50** split on profits beyond +15%. The demo can scale slowly: +25% every 60 days of consistent profitability, capped at 2× starting size. Payouts in real USDC from the fee pool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track B — Real-capital vault opens" icon="vault">
    A new LP-funded vault opens in parallel — seeded by HyroTrader and open to LP deposits from minute one. Real Bybit trading. Split: **80% trader / 15% LPs / 5% protocol**. High-water-mark protected, per-LP cost basis.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Note>
You don't lose the demo. You **gain** an additional LP-funded capital layer alongside it. Scaling is by performance — not by an artificial graduation event.
</Note>

## The three stages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stage A — Challenge purchase & evaluation">
    Pay the evaluation fee in USDC. Trade the demo under rules (4% daily DD, 6% trailing, 10% target, 5 minimum days). Pass → Stage B. Fail → demo canceled, no refund.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stage B — Funded demo, Tier 1 (0% to +15%)">
    Trade the funded demo. Request payouts via Phantom. Split: **80% trader / 20% firm** on all Tier 1 profits. When cumulative demo profit crosses +15%, the dual-track unlocks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stage C — Dual-track unlock at +15%">
    Two things happen simultaneously, and the demo is not canceled: the demo continues at Tier 2 (50/50 incremental) **and** a real-capital vault opens in parallel with firm seed and LP deposits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The economics at each stage

| Stage | Surface | Split | Capital |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (0 → +15%) | Funded demo | 80% trader / 20% firm | Simulated |
| Tier 2 (+15% onward) | Funded demo | 50% trader / 50% firm (incremental) | Simulated |
| Real-capital vault | LP-funded vault | 80% trader / 15% LPs / 5% protocol | Real (Bybit) |

<Info>
**Firm seed for the vault** is 10% of starting demo size — so a 25k demo opens a vault seeded with 2.5k, a 50k with 5k, a 100k with 10k. LPs deposit on top of that seed.
</Info>

## Why this design

Traders on legacy FX prop firms try to extract payouts as fast as possible, because the firm offers nothing beyond a one-shot funded account. The dual-track changes the incentive: traders who pass can build a verifiable on-chain track record, attract LP capital, and scale trading as a career. That shifts behavior toward longer holding periods, better risk management, and higher trading quality — which compounds into LP returns.
