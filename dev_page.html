Hyro Protocol

On-chain crypto prop trading. Verifiable. Scalable. Settlement on Solana.

Hyro Protocol is the on-chain layer for crypto prop trading. Verified traders manage capital pools on Solana, liquidity providers deposit USDC to underwrite them, and every payout, share, and NAV update settles on-chain. It is built and operated by HyroTrader — the first crypto prop firm with native Bybit integration, operating since 2020.

What Makes It Different

Smart-contract rules

Drawdown limits, profit targets, and risk parameters are on-chain policy programs. Rules cannot change mid-evaluation.

Automated on-chain payouts

Paid in USDC by smart contract. Every payout has a verifiable TXID.

LP-funded scale

Funded account size scales with LP demand, not the firm’s balance sheet.

The dual-track model

Hit +15% and a real-capital vault opens in parallel — your demo is never canceled.
