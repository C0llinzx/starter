Hyro Protocol is the on-chain layer for crypto prop trading. Verified traders manage capital pools on Solana. Liquidity providers (LPs) deposit USDC to underwrite traders and earn a share of profits. Every payout, every share, every NAV update settles on-chain.

It is built and operated by **HyroTrader** — the first crypto prop firm with native Bybit integration, operating since 2020, with 30,000+ traders served and $5M+ in payouts.
