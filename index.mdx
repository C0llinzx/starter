Hyro Protocol is the on-chain layer for crypto prop trading. Verified traders manage capital pools on Solana, liquidity providers deposit USDC to underwrite them, and every payout, share, and NAV update settles on-chain. It is built and operated by HyroTrader — the first crypto prop firm with native Bybit integration, operating since 2020.
