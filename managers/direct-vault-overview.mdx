A Direct Vault lets verified managers raise LP capital against an existing track record — no challenge, no evaluation. Your proven performance is the qualification.

<Info>
Direct Vault applications open to external managers in **Phase 2 (Q1 2027)**. At launch, Direct Vaults are limited to hand-selected HyroTrader graduates.
</Info>

## Why a Direct Vault

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="LP capital on your terms" icon="sliders-horizontal">
    You set the performance fee (10–30%), lockup, min deposit, visibility, and access rules at creation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-venue" icon="network">
    Not locked to a single platform. Bybit today; more CEXes and on-chain DEXes as integration completes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Position privacy" icon="eye-off">
    Aggregate-only mode is the default — your live position book stays hidden from copy-trading bots.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On-chain track record" icon="link">
    Your performance is verifiable per LP cost basis, building a portable, provable history.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Direct Vault vs Challenge Vault

| | Challenge Vault | Direct Vault |
|---|---|---|
| Who | Traders without a track record | Verified managers with one |
| Entry | Pay a fee, pass evaluation | Apply with track record, no fee |
| Economics | Fixed 20% perf fee | Configurable 10–30% |
| Fee split | 80% trader / 15% LPs / 5% protocol | 90% manager / 5% protocol / 5% insurance |
| Visibility default | Public | Aggregate-only |
| Available | Q4 2026 | Q1 2027 (external) |
