The Challenge Vault is the path for traders **without** an existing track record. Pay an entry fee in USDC, trade under smart-contract rules, pass the evaluation, and get funded.

## Structure

Hyro's Challenge Vault uses a **one-step structure** by default: a single evaluation phase leads directly to a funded account. (A two-step structure with a 10% / 15% split is also configurable.)

## Account sizes

| Size | Simulated balance |
|---|---|
| Starter | 25,000 USDC |
| Standard | 50,000 USDC |
| Pro | 100,000 USDC |

You pay the evaluation fee in USDC. Gas is sponsored — you never need SOL.

## What's locked at purchase

When you buy a challenge, the **rulebook version is written on-chain** and pinned to your challenge. The rules you signed up for are the rules you're evaluated against — even if the rulebook updates later.

[How the versioned rulebook works →](/resources/decentralization)

## What you get when you pass

<Check>A funded demo account at Tier 1 (80% trader / 20% firm), paid in real USDC</Check>
<Check>The dual-track path: at +15%, a real-capital LP-funded vault opens in parallel</Check>
<Check>An on-chain track record you carry across vaults and seasons</Check>

## Challenge Vault vs Direct Vault

| | Challenge Vault | Direct Vault |
|---|---|---|
| Who | Traders without a track record | Verified managers with one |
| Entry | Pay a fee, pass evaluation | Apply with track record, no fee |
| Economics | Fixed 20% perf fee | Configurable 10–30% |
| Available | Q4 2026 | Q1 2027 (external) |
