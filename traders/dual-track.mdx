At **+15%** cumulative demo profit, a real-capital, LP-funded vault opens in parallel with your Tier 2 demo. This is where you scale past any firm balance-sheet ceiling.

## What opens

<Steps>
  <Step title="A vault PDA on Solana">
    Auto-created on-chain. Your execution rights are constrained by policy — you never hold custody of the capital.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Firm seed">
    HyroTrader seeds the vault with **10% of your starting demo size** — 2.5k for a 25k demo, 5k for 50k, 10k for 100k.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A new Bybit sub-account">
    Provisioned for real trading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="LP deposits open">
    LPs can deposit on top of the seed from minute one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The split

On the real-capital vault, the performance fee splits:

| Recipient | Share |
|---|---|
| **You (trader)** | 80% |
| LPs (carry) | 15% |
| Protocol | 5% |

The fee is **high-water-mark protected** with a **per-LP cost basis** — so an LP is never charged twice on the same gain, and each LP's fee is measured against their own entry NAV.

There is no "graduation event." You don't lose the demo — you gain an additional LP-funded capital layer. Scaling is by performance.
</Note>

## What LPs see

When your vault opens, LPs can evaluate it before depositing. These metrics are always public and cannot be hidden:

- NAV (updated ~every 60 seconds), total AUM, drawdown
- Realized returns and payouts
- Performance fee rate
- Your skin-in-the-game stake

Your **live positions** can be public, aggregate-only, or time-delayed — your choice — to protect strategy from copy-trading bots. [Visibility modes →](/lps/visibility-privacy)
