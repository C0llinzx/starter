This tutorial takes you through one full, successful path: sign in, buy a challenge, pass it, and request your first payout. Follow it once and you'll understand the whole trader surface.

<Info>
This walkthrough describes the launch flow. Challenge Vaults go live from Q4 2026. On devnet today, withdrawal timelocks are compressed to ~1 hour for testing.
</Info>

## 1. Sign in

Go to [hyro.network](https://hyro.network) and choose how to sign in:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email (easiest)">
    Enter your email. A Solana wallet is generated for you automatically — no browser extension, no seed phrase to manage up front. You keep private-key control and can export to Phantom whenever you want.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Phantom (self-custody)">
    Connect your existing Phantom wallet. Recommended for serious managers who want full self-custody from day one.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 2. Pick a size and pay

Choose your account size and pay the evaluation fee in USDC.

| Size | Simulated balance |
|---|---|
| Starter | 25,000 |
| Standard | 50,000 |
| Pro | 100,000 |

When you pay:
- The fee is recorded on-chain and lands in the **fee vault**.
- The **rulebook version** is pinned to your challenge on-chain. The rules you signed up for are the rules you'll be evaluated against — even if the rulebook updates later.

<Tip>
You don't pay gas. Hyro sponsors the Solana transaction.
</Tip>

## 3. Connect Bybit and start trading

Connect your Bybit account. Hyro provisions a demo account, and you trade under these rules:

| Rule | Limit |
|---|---|
| Daily drawdown | 4% |
| Max loss (trailing) | 6% |
| Profit target | 10% |
| Minimum trading days | 5 |
| Profit concentration cap | 40% |

A Rust engine monitors your account continuously. An oracle posts your equity and the pass/fail verdict to Solana.

<Warning>
The **5-day minimum** is deliberate. It forces multi-session demonstration of risk discipline and filters the single-trade exploit attempts that target firms with no minimum. Don't try to pass on one high-conviction trade.
</Warning>

<Check>
**Pass:** you hit +10% without breaking a drawdown limit, across at least 5 trading days. You move to the funded demo at Tier 1.
</Check>

<Warning>
**Fail:** you break a drawdown limit or violate a rule. The demo is canceled and the fee is not refunded.
</Warning>

## 4. Trade the funded demo (Tier 1)

Once funded, you keep **80%** of all profits from 0% up to +15%, paid in real USDC.

To request a payout:
1. Submit the request via Phantom.
2. The contract checks eligibility on-chain.
3. USDC transfers from the **payout vault** to your wallet — with a TXID you can verify on Solana explorer.

<Note>
In v1, payouts go through a manual approval queue. Later this becomes an auto-approval with a safety window.
</Note>

## 5. Cross +15% — the dual-track unlocks

When your cumulative demo profit crosses **+15%**, two things happen at once and your demo is **not** canceled:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Demo continues (Tier 2)" icon="repeat">
    Split shifts to 50/50 on incremental profit. Demo can scale +25% every 60 days, capped at 2×.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-capital vault opens" icon="vault">
    Seeded by the firm, open to LP deposits, trading real money on Bybit. Split: 80% you / 15% LPs / 5% protocol.
  </Card>
</Columns>
