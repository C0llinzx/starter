<Info>
Direct Vault applications open to external managers in **Phase 2 (Q1 2027)**. At launch (Phase 1), Direct Vaults are limited to hand-selected HyroTrader graduates.
</Info>

The Direct Vault path skips the challenge entirely. Your **existing track record** is the qualification.

## Who it's for

- Individual professional traders
- Funds, partnerships, RIAs
- Algorithmic trading desks
- Market makers

## Eligibility

| Requirement | Value |
|---|---|
| Verifiable trading history | 6 months minimum, continuous |
| Track record import | Read-only API key **or** cryptographically signed snapshot |
| Realized PnL | $50,000+ over the verifiable period |
| Max drawdown | Under 15% trailing 6 months |
| Risk-adjusted metric | Sharpe > 1.0 or comparable |
| Identity check | KYC (individuals) · KYB (entities) |
| Geographic eligibility | US retail, UK retail, sanctioned territories excluded |

## Application process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply online">
    Submit track record, strategy, and identity documents at hyro.network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admin review (1–2 weeks)">
    Track-record verification and strategy assessment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="KYC / KYB (1–2 weeks, parallel)">
    Processed by a qualified third-party provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategy interview (~1 hour)">
    Video call with the Head of Trading or a designated reviewer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision">
    Final approval or structured feedback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Typical total: 3–5 weeks** from application to vault opening.

## Vault creation conditions

On approval, you configure your vault:

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Manager stake | 5% of expected initial TVL, locked 3 months, visible on-chain |
| Initial TVL cap | $50K – $250K, sized to track-record strength |
| Performance fee | Your choice, 10–30% |
| Drawdown limits | Max-DD shutdown 5–20%, max leverage 1×–20× (defaults: 8% DD, 10×) |
| Visibility | Aggregate-only (default) · Public · Time-delayed 7 days |
| Access | Public (default) · Private link · Whitelist |
| Min LP deposit | Default $100, up to $50,000 |
| LP lockup | Default none, up to 30 days |

## Manager economics

| Your perf fee | You net | Protocol | Insurance | LPs keep |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 10% | 9% of profit | 0.5% | 0.5% | 90% of profit |
| 20% | 18% of profit | 1% | 1% | 80% of profit |
| 30% | 27% of profit | 1.5% | 1.5% | 70% of profit |

This matches standard hedge-fund and crypto-fund economics — a manager running a 30% perf-fee fund externally can run a Hyro Direct Vault on the same terms.
