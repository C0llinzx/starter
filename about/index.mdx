Hyro Protocol is the on-chain layer for crypto prop trading, built and operated by HyroTrader.

## Mission

Crypto prop trading is a real market with real problems — refused payouts, opaque accounting, a structural conflict of interest, and an artificial ceiling on how far a trader can scale. Hyro Protocol exists to fix this by moving the entire model on-chain: smart-contract rules that can't change mid-evaluation, automated USDC payouts anyone can verify, and LP-funded vaults that let traders scale past any firm's balance sheet.

The launch product is crypto prop trading — chosen because HyroTrader has five years of operational data to prove the model fastest. But the protocol is built as a generalized on-chain vault primitive: the same architecture that funds prop traders today is designed to support funds, asset managers, algorithmic desks, and market makers as it matures.

We tell you exactly what is centralized and exactly when it changes. [See our decentralization status →](/resources/decentralization)
