The Hyro Protocol payout vault is a PDA on Solana with its USDC balance queryable on-chain at any time.

## The live reserve widget

A widget on [hyro.network](https://hyro.network) displays:

- The current payout vault balance
- Total payouts processed
- A feed of recent payout transactions

All linked to Solana explorer for verification.

## Why this matters

This is a proof-of-reserves pattern that post-FTX crypto users explicitly look for. It is structurally different from claiming "we have funds":

<Note>
Anyone can verify the on-chain balance independently. You don't have to trust a statement — you can query the PDA directly.
</Note>

## How to verify independently

The payout vault is a Solana PDA. You can:

1. Read its USDC token account balance directly via any Solana RPC.
2. Cross-check the recent payout feed against on-chain transaction history.
3. Confirm each payout TXID resolves on a Solana explorer.

## Historical context

HyroTrader has paid out **$5M+** to traders since 2020. Payouts from Phase 1 onward are on-chain by default; the historical total is disclosed transparently but not retroactively claimed as on-chain (the infrastructure wasn't live then).
